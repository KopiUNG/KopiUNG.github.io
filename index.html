Our Story

KopiUNG is a business operating in the Coffee Shop sector which is located at Jalan Raya Sesetan No. 65, South Denpasar District, KopiUNG was founded in 2019. Where KopiUNG means coffee water. where the focus is on coffee drinks. And almost all coffee raw materials come from various regions in Indonesia and also various regions abroad. Not only coffee, but also various types of non-coffee drinks. which is intended for customers who don't really like coffee. And this Coffee Shop also sells various kinds of light to heavy food. And KopiUNG also has an interior design with a minimalist concept using wooden materials and decoration with various types of ornamental plants. which is even more of an attraction.
